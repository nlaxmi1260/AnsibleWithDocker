Ansible Conditions

  1. In Ansible conditions are defined with when keyword.
  2. In Ansible we can give multiple conditions as list
    when:
      - ansible_os_family == "RedHat"
      - ansible_distribution_version == "7"
  3. In Ansible we can give muliple conditions with and keyword when:
      - ansible_os_family == "RedHat" and ansible_distribution_version == "7"
  4. In Ansible we can give condition with loop
    when:
      - item == "apple"
      loop:
        - apple
        - orange
        - banana
  5. Ansible facts are the facts about managed nodes
